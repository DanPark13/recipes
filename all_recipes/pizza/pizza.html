Simple Personal Pizza Recipe

Utensils

  • Baking Sheet
  • Parchment Paper

    • You can also use a foil as an alternative, but parchment paper keeps the pizza bread from sticking to the baking sheet.

  • Oven

Ingredients

  • Pre-Made Tomato Sauce

    • You don't need anything fancy. Use one you have similar to the one I linked above. It literally doesn't matter as long as it can hold pasta and hot water together at the same time. It don't even need to have a lid. Also you don't need to buy more sauce if you have tomato sauce already. Pizza sauce is a scam.

  • Shredded Mozzerela Cheese

    • Or any kind of shredded cheese

  • Pizza Bread

    • Preferably pizza bread since it bakes nicely, but any bread (as long as it fits your baking sheet) will do

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven to 450°
  2. Put parchment paper on the cooking sheet
  3. Place pizza bread on the cooking sheet
  4. Spread the tomato sauce on the pizza and sprinkle cheese generously on the pizza
  5. Optional: Put additional ingredients such as meats and veggies on the pizza
  6. Place pizza with cooking sheet into the oven
  7. I usually wait about 10-15 minutes, but be sure to check on the pizza regularly to ensure the cheese melts. Undercooking keeps the cheese from melting and overcooking burns the pizza
  8. Take out the pizza, and enjoy!
